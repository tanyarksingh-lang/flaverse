Our Story

Every Dish Begins With
A Passion For Great Taste

Flaverse brings together beloved Indian flavours and modern favourites under one roof. From rich Mughlai curries and aromatic biryanis to comforting Chinese and seafood specialties, every dish is prepared with care, consistency, and attention to flavour. Based in Kothrud, Pune—we're a cloud kitchen built for the people who know that great food doesn't need a fancy address.
